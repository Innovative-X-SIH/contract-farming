About the Project

This contract farming project connects farmers and buyers to form mutually beneficial agreements. Farmers get access to a guaranteed market, and buyers receive high-quality produce with consistent pricing and delivery.

How It Works

Step 1

Farmers and buyers register and provide necessary details.

Step 2

Contract negotiation and agreement based on crops and quantity.

Step 3

Farmers grow crops with the help of modern tools and technology.

Step 4

Delivery of crops and payment after successful completion.

Benefits of Contract Farming

Guaranteed Market

Farmers have access to a ready market for their produce, ensuring consistent income.

High-Quality Produce

Buyers get assured quality crops and competitive pricing through contracts.

Technology Support

Farmers receive support for modern technology and farming techniques.
